## Core Frameworks & Mental Models

Here's an organized glossary of key terms from the provided chapters:

- **Accuracy**: Refers to how often a model makes correct predictions or decisions (Ch 6).
- **Data Deduplication**: The process of removing duplicate data entries to improve efficiency and performance (Ch 8 & Ch 10).
- **Model Distillation**: A technique where a small model is trained to mimic the behavior of a larger, more complex model (Ch 23).
- **Optimization Techniques**: Methods used to improve model performance, accuracy, or efficiency (Ch 6 & Ch 9).
- **Semantic Similarity**: A measure of how similar two pieces of text are in meaning (Ch 19).
