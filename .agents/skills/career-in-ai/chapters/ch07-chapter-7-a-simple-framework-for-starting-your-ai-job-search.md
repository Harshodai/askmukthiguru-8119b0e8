# Chapter 7: A Simple Framework for Starting Your AI Job Search

## Core Idea  
This chapter introduces a strategic approach to beginning an AI job search by leveraging frameworks, actionable principles, and techniques.

## Frameworks Introduced  
- **AI Job Search Pyramid**:  
  - When to use: To prioritize actions in the job search process.  
  - How: Focus on core elements like AI Strategy, Skills, Tools, Networking, and Feedback.  

- **The 5 Whys**:  
  - When to use: To define roles and responsibilities during an AI job search.  
  - How: Ask "Why" five times to uncover underlying reasons for decisions.

## Key Concepts  
- **AI Job Search Pyramid**: A visual model guiding the prioritization of actions in an AI-related job search.  

- **The 5 Whys**: A questioning technique to dig deeper into decision-making processes.

## Mental Models  
- Use the AI Job Search Pyramid when you need a structured approach to prioritize your job search efforts.  
- Think of The 5 Whys as a tool to uncover root causes in defining roles and responsibilities.

## Anti-patterns  
- **Aimless Job Search**: Fails because it lacks direction and focus, leading to wasted time and resources.
