# Universal Intelligence

*Teacher: [[sri-preethaji]] • [[sri-krishnaji]]*

The Sun God teaches: the universal intelligence is the soul or the being of the universe. It permeates the visible and the invisible. The field of divine intelligence is innate in every sentient being and pervades every aspect of nature.

This is not a metaphor. It is a description of reality as the mystics see it.

## The Nature of the Field

Universal intelligence is not a distant force that occasionally intervenes. It is the very substance of existence — closer than your own breath, more intimate than your thoughts. It pervades:

- The visible universe — stars, planets, bodies, matter
- The invisible universe — thoughts, feelings, consciousness itself
- Every sentient being — not as a visitor but as the being's own deepest nature

## The Law of Response

This is the most important aspect of the teaching: universal intelligence responds to your state of being. Not to your words, not to your actions, not to your prayers or rituals or sacrifices. To your state.

When you live in a beautiful state of love, peace, joy, or compassion, you are connected. When you live in suffering states — anger, fear, hurt, loneliness — you are disconnected.

The disconnection is not a punishment. It is a natural consequence, like pulling a plant out of soil and wondering why it does not grow.

## The Celebration of Oneness

International Yoga Day is a celebration of this universal intelligence — the spirit of yoga, which is oneness. Oneness of humanity, oneness of all life. This celebration is for everyone: pro or novice, believer or non-believer. The universal intelligence does not discriminate. It only responds to state.

## Application

When you feel disconnected — from yourself, from others, from life — do not try to reconnect by force. Do not pray louder or practice harder. Instead, check your state. Find the stress, the fear, the hurt. Tend to it with the serene mind practice. Return to the beautiful state. The connection restores itself.
