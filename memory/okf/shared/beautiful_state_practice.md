# Beautiful State Practice

In 1274 AD, Kublai Khan invaded Japan with 20,000 soldiers on 500 ships. Japan was unprepared. As the Mongol fleet reached the shore, a storm stretching from horizon to horizon rose up and tore the fleet apart. The Japanese called it *Kamikaze* — the Divine Wind.

Seven years later, the Mongols tried again. A typhoon, completely out of season, destroyed the entire fleet.

Coincidence? Miracle? Luck?

Sri Preethaji [[sri-preethaji]] and Sri Krishnaji [[sri-krishnaji]] call it something else: synchronicity.

## The Science of Synchronicity

The universe is not a collection of separate blocks of matter. It is a field — an interconnected web of energy and consciousness. When your inner state is aligned, the field responds. Events that seem random from the outside begin to arrange themselves in meaningful patterns.

Students of this teaching report that synchronicities become a regular feature of their lives:
- The right person appears at the right moment
- The needed information arrives just when they were seeking an answer
- Opportunities emerge as if placed in their path
- Inner struggle dissolves into a sense of being carried

## Two Requirements

But synchronicities are not random gifts. They require two conditions to be met in your consciousness:

**1. A beautiful inner state.** The state of being is the signal you broadcast. A stressed state broadcasts stress and receives stress-matched experiences. A beautiful state broadcasts coherence and receives coherence-matched experiences.

**2. A personal connection with universal intelligence.** Most people think of universal intelligence as an impersonal force field. The mystics know differently: this intelligence can be related to as a being — as a friend, a mother, a father, a beloved. When you experience this personal connection, the field does not just respond to your state — it responds to *you*.

## What This Teaching Does Not Say

This is not a formula for manipulating the universe to get what you want. The beautiful state is not a technique for material gain. The personal connection with universal intelligence is not a transaction. The synchronicities that arise from this state serve the well-being of life, not the aggrandisement of the ego. A selfish prayer from a stressed state changes nothing.

## Application

The practice is simple but not easy: live in the beautiful state and relate to universal intelligence as a presence. Do this not to get things, but because this is what life is when lived at its full depth. The synchronicities will come — not as rewards, but as the natural expression of a life in alignment.
