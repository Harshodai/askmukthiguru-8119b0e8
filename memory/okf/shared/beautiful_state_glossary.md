# Glossary of Core Terms

*Teachers: [[sri-preethaji]] • [[sri-krishnaji]]*

- [[sri-preethaji/beautiful_state|Beautiful State]] — The no-stress state of connection, joy, love,
  compassion, vitality, and passion. The default when not in stress; the
  intended ground of all action and relationship.
- **Stressful State** — Anger, fear, hurt, loneliness, frustration — any state
  rooted in self-centric preoccupation and disconnection from others.
- **Self-centric Thinking** — Obsessive, incessant preoccupation with oneself.
  The root cause that keeps suffering alive long after a stressful event ends.
- [[shared/inner_truth_and_being|Inner Truth]] — The insight that all lingering emotional suffering arises
  from self-centric thinking. Seeing this is the antidote to suffering and the
  way back to a beautiful state.
- [[sri-krishnaji/serene_mind_practice|Serene Mind]] — A 3-minute meditation practice for returning to calm:
  conscious breathing, emotion recognition, observing thought direction, and
  holding attention on a flame at the eyebrow center.
- **Connection** — The mark of the beautiful state. In it you feel another's
  pain and act from empathy, not from ideal or guilt. In stress, you are
  disconnected even amid people.
- **Presence** — Being engaged with the present rather than entangled in past
  or future. In a beautiful state there is no compulsive illumination of the
  past or anxiety over the future; the mind is uncluttered.
