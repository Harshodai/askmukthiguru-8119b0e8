# Finding Truth in Chaos

Peace is not the brief respite between two chaotic events in life. Sri Preethaji [[sri-preethaji]] and Sri Krishnaji [[sri-krishnaji]] open this teaching with a stark diagnosis: humanity faces a fourth destiny. On one side is the written-down abysmal depth of global crisis — mental health, economic collapse, pandemic, climate destruction. On the other side is a possibility that depends entirely on whether millions of us move from separation-driven consciousness to oneness consciousness.

## The Revolution Called Awakening

The separation you feel from another is an illusion. You have to disidentify from it — like waking up from a bad dream and laughing at it. The truth is that we are one.

When millions bring their intention together — infusing the field with dreams, hearts, and the highest possibility of peace — something shifts in the fabric of collective consciousness. This is not metaphor. The science now validates what the mystics have always known: we are not separate blocks of matter. We are the field. Every atom of every molecule of every cell in our bodies is constantly emerging and collapsing into the field. We *are* the field.

## The Responsive Field

The field knows only peace-and-coherence or chaos-and-anger. When we try to create change through anger, the field cannot respond with peace. It mirrors what we feed it. The question is not "What do we do?" but "What do we feed the field?"

This is the deepest truth of the teaching: attempting to change the world from a state of anger is self-defeating. The means must match the end. Peace cannot be achieved through non-peaceful means.

## The Ultimate Identity

We are not separate blocks of matter. We are not bodies that are born and die. We are the river of life itself. We are one consciousness — inseparable from every person, every tree, every animal, from Mother Earth and this universe.

Awakening to peace is the dharma of every human being. Millions have to wake up from the daydreams of samsara. This is Mukti — liberation. On this liberated state stands the great enlightened states of God-realisation and Self-realisation.

## Application

When chaos swirls around you, do not look for peace in the external situation. It will not be found there. Look within: *What am I feeding the field right now?* If the answer is anger, fear, or resistance, the first step is not to fix the world — it is to shift your state. From the shifted state, right action arises naturally.

## Related Concepts
- **Atma**: The true Self — pure consciousness, unchanging, untouched by the chaos of worldly experience
- **Moksha**: Liberation from the illusion of separation, which is the foundation of all true peace
- **Separation Consciousness**: The mistaken identity that causes all conflict, both inner and outer
