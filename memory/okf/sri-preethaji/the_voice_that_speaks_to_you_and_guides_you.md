# The Voice That Speaks to You and Guides You

Sri Preethaji [[sri-preethaji]] teaches that there is a voice within every person that speaks truth and offers guidance. This is not the voice of conscience in the moral sense. It is not the internalised voice of parents or society. It is something more fundamental — an intelligence that knows what is needed, not what is expected.

## Why You Cannot Hear It

The voice is always present. It does not speak only to the worthy or the advanced. It speaks to everyone, always. The reason most people do not hear it is not that the voice is silent — it is that they are not in a state where they can listen.

The beautiful state is the condition for hearing. When you are in stress, anxiety, anger, or fear, the mind is too loud. The voice is speaking, but you cannot perceive it over the noise of your own suffering.

## How to Enter the Listening State

The voice becomes accessible when you move into deep stillness — the same stillness described throughout the teachings. It is cultivated through meditation, kriya, and the consistent practice of returning to the beautiful state.

In that stillness, the voice is not heard as words (though it can take the form of words). It is more often felt as a knowing — a quiet certainty about a decision, a gentle pull toward or away from something, a sense of what is aligned and what is not.

## The Awakening of Intuition

When you consistently dwell in the beautiful state, intuition awakens. This is not the same as guessing or hoping. Intuition, in this teaching, is a reliable faculty — as reliable as sight or hearing, once it is developed. The voice becomes clearer over time, and you learn to trust it because it proves itself accurate.

## Application

When you face a decision, do not immediately consult your analytical mind. First, return to the beautiful state. From that place, ask the question silently. Notice what arises — not what you *think* you should do, but what you *know*. The answer may arrive immediately, or it may take time. Trust the process.

## Related Concepts
- **Beautiful State** ([[sri-preethaji/beautiful_state]]): The receptive condition in which inner guidance becomes audible
- **Inner Stillness**: The quiet ground from which the voice can be perceived clearly
- **Awake Intuition**: The developed capacity to receive guidance from the voice with reliability
- **Kriya**: Meditative practices that deepen the stillness necessary for hearing
