# The Voice That Speaks to You and Guides You

## Summary
This teaching explores the nature of inner guidance and the voice that directs individuals toward their highest good. Grounded in the O&O Academy's framework of the Beautiful State, the teaching emphasizes that accessing this guiding voice requires entering a state of deep stillness and connection beyond ordinary consciousness.

## Key Teachings
- The voice that guides is accessible when one enters the Beautiful State, a condition of profound inner peace and connection free from suffering (Sri Preethaji explains...).
- This guidance emerges not through analytical thinking but through cultivated stillness and meditation practices, including kriya.
- The Beautiful State awakens intuition and provides help at every step of the journey, allowing individuals to hear and follow their inner compass.

## Quotes
> "The voice that speaks to you and guides you is always present, waiting for you to enter the stillness where you can hear it." — Sri Krishnaji

## Related Concepts
- Beautiful State: A state of connection, peace, and inner joy, free from suffering, where inner guidance becomes accessible
- Inner Stillness: The quietened consciousness that allows the guiding voice to be heard clearly
- Awake Intuition: The awakened inner wisdom that emerges when one dwells in the Beautiful State
- Kriya: A meditation practice that helps cultivate the stillness necessary to hear the guiding voice
