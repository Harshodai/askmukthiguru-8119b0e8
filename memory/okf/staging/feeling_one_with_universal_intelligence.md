# Feeling One with Universal Intelligence

## Summary
This teaching from the Mukthi Guru platform explores the concept of unity with universal intelligence. A Mukthi Guru serves as a teacher of liberation and enlightenment, guiding seekers toward experiencing oneness with the universal consciousness that transcends individual limitation.

## Key Teachings
- The Mukthi Guru platform is focused on the teachings of liberation, where a Mukthi Guru is defined as a teacher of enlightenment
- The path to feeling one with universal intelligence is taught through the spiritual guidance offered on this platform
- Liberation (Moksha) is the core doctrine taught, pointing toward ultimate unity with universal intelligence

## Related Concepts
- Mukthi Guru: A teacher of liberation or enlightenment
- Moksha: Liberation; the state of oneness with universal intelligence
- Deeksha: The spiritual transfer of knowledge from guru to disciple that facilitates awakening
