# Intuitive Knowledge of True Nature

## Summary
This teaching explores the nature of knowledge, suggesting it isn't solely intellectual but also intuitive and heart-based. This intuitive knowledge reveals a fundamental truth about our existence, connecting us to our "true nature" and potentially linked to an evolutionary understanding of our place in the universe.

## Key Teachings
- The source of true knowledge lies in intuition and the heart, rather than solely through intellectual means. ("ways known intuitively is true in our hearts")
- This intuitive knowledge reveals a fundamental truth about our existence and our "true nature." ("The Nature of this Knowledge")

## Quotes
> "ways known intuitively is true in our hearts" — Sri Krishnaji

## Related Concepts
- Intuitive Knowledge: Knowledge gained through direct insight, feeling, or understanding, rather than through reasoning or logic.
- True Nature: A core concept referring to the essential, unchanging essence of a being, often explored in spiritual traditions.
- Evolution: The process of gradual change and development, potentially encompassing spiritual growth and understanding.
