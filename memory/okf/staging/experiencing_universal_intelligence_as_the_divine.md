# Experiencing Universal Intelligence as the Divine

# Experiencing Universal Intelligence as the Divine

## Summary
This teaching explains that universal intelligence can be perceived through the lens of one's own mystic tradition—as the various gods and sages. When one experiences the divine as the unmanifest, it is understood as a field of universal intelligence that is everywhere and is everything. This represents one of two ways to experience the divine.

## Key Teachings
- You can experience the divine in two ways: as the manifest or as the unmanifest.
- When you experience the divine as the unmanifest, you see it as a field of universal intelligence.
- This intelligence is everywhere and is everything.
- You can see the universal intelligence as the various gods and the sages of your mystic tradition.

## Quotes
> "You can experience the divine either as the manifest or the unmanifest." — Sri Preethaji

> "This intelligence is everywhere, is everything." — Sri Preethaji

## Related Concepts
- **Manifest**: One of two ways to experience the divine; the opposite of the unmanifest.
- **Unmanifest**: The aspect of divine experience described as a field of universal intelligence.
- **Universal Intelligence**: The underlying intelligence that is everywhere and everything, which can be perceived through mystic traditions as gods and sages.
