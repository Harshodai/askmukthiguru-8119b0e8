# You Feel One with Your Universal Intelligence

## Summary
This teaching explores the concept of experiencing oneness with universal intelligence within the Mukthi Guru spiritual framework. A Mukthi Guru is described as a teacher of liberation or enlightenment, guiding practitioners toward transcendent states of consciousness where individual awareness merges with universal wisdom.

## Key Teachings
- The state of feeling one with universal intelligence represents a core aspect of spiritual liberation (moksha) as taught through the Mukthi Guru platform.
- Mukthi Guru serves as a teacher guiding seekers toward enlightenment and the realization of unity with universal consciousness.

## Related Concepts
- moksha: The concept of liberation or enlightenment, freedom from the cycle of rebirth and union with universal consciousness
- guru: A spiritual teacher who provides guidance on the path to enlightenment
- deeksha: Spiritual guidance or transfer of knowledge from guru to disciple
