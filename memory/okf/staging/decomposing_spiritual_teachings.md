# Decomposing Spiritual Teachings

## Summary
This teaching focuses on the process of analyzing spiritual teachings. The core task involves decomposing a spiritual teaching into independent, self-contained propositions. This process aims to identify the main theme or topic within the teaching.

## Key Teachings
- The core action being described is *decomposition* or *analysis* of spiritual teachings. — Sri Krishnaji
- The goal of this analysis is to find the "main theme or topic." — Sri Krishnaji

## Quotes
> "Core Task: Decompose a spiritual teaching..." — Sri Krishnaji

## Related Concepts
- Decomposition: The act of breaking down a complex idea into smaller, more manageable parts.
- Analysis: The process of examining something methodically and in detail.
