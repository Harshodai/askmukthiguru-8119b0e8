# Universal Intelligence in Deities

## Summary
The divine, understood as universal intelligence, can be experienced in two primary ways: as the manifest or the unmanifest. When perceived as the unmanifest, it reveals itself as a pervasive field of intelligence that is everywhere and is everything. This universal intelligence can also be seen through the lens of one's own mystic tradition, where the various gods and sages are expressions of this same fundamental reality.

## Key Teachings
- The divine as universal intelligence can be experienced in two primary ways: as the manifest or the unmanifest.
- When perceived as the unmanifest, universal intelligence reveals itself as a pervasive field of intelligence that is everywhere and is everything.
- Through the lens of one's own mystic tradition, the various gods and sages are expressions of this same fundamental reality.
