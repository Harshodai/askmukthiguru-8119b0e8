# Claiming the Inner State

# Claiming the Inner State

## Summary
This teaching explores the fundamental importance of the inner state and the spiritual goal of claiming and proclaiming it. Sri Preethaji and Sri Krishnaji emphasize that the inner state is the foundation of all true transformation, and that practitioners are called to actively claim this state as their own reality.

## Key Teachings
- The goal is to claim the inner state as your own.
- The goal is to proclaim the inner state to the world.
- The inner state is the most important aspect of spiritual practice.

## Quotes
> "The goal is to claim." — Sri Preethaji

> "The goal is to proclaim." — Sri Krishnaji

## Related Concepts
- Beautiful State: A state of connection, peace, and inner joy, free from suffering, which emerges when one claims the inner state.
- Inner Stillness: A state of calm awareness that supports the claiming of the inner state.
- Meditation: A practice that helps cultivate and claim the inner state.
