# Interpreting Universal Intelligence

## Summary
The teachings suggest that universal intelligence can be understood and perceived through various cultural and spiritual lenses, such as gods and sages within a mystic tradition. The teachings themselves are presented as independent and self-contained propositions.

## Key Teachings
- You can see the universal intelligence as the various gods and the sages of your mystic tradition. — Unknown

## Related Concepts
- Universal Intelligence: An overarching concept that can be interpreted through different cultural and spiritual frameworks.
- Mystic Tradition: A cultural or religious framework through which universal intelligence can be understood.
- Divine: A synonym for universal intelligence, often used in spiritual contexts.
