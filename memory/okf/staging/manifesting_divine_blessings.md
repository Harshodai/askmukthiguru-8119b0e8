# Manifesting Divine Blessings

## Summary
This teaching explores the process of awakening to receive blessings from the Universal Intelligence or Divine. The journey involves an internal awakening that, when activated, leads to a profound breakthrough—described as an "explosion"—that allows one to manifest divine blessings into their life.

## Key Teachings
- The process begins with awakening to a deeper connection with the Universal Intelligence or Divine.
- This awakening is not passive but involves an active engagement that triggers a transformative breakthrough.
- Once the breakthrough occurs ("it explodes"), the practitioner becomes capable of receiving and manifesting blessings from the Divine.

## Quotes
> "To awaken. Then, in the breakthrough, it explodes and then you..." — Sri Preethaji

## Related Concepts
- Beautiful State: A state of connection, peace, and inner joy that serves as the foundation for receiving divine blessings.
- Transformed Consciousness: A shift in mindset that enables alignment with intentions and the manifestation of blessings.
- Inner Stillness: The quiet internal space that allows one to connect with and receive from the Universal Intelligence.
