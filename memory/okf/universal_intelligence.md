## Summary
This teaching explores the concept of Universal Intelligence through the lens of oneness, delivered during International Yoga Day. The Sun God (Surya) narrates an ancient Hindu mythological legend involving Hanuman and the Sun God, using this story to illuminate the spiritual principle that the spirit of yoga is fundamentally about oneness.

## Key Teachings
- The spirit of yoga is oneness, which includes the oneness of humanity and the oneness of all life. (The Sun God teaches this through the mythological narration)
- This celebration is for everyone, regardless of their skill level or belief, emphasizing the universal nature of this teaching.

## Quotes
> "The spirit of yoga is oneness, which includes the oneness of humanity and the oneness of all life." — The Sun God
