# Inner Truth and Being

Sri Preethaji and Sri Krishnaji offer a simple statement that contains the whole of their teaching:

*"Your truth is a statement of truth."*

This is not a logical proposition. It is a mantra — a tool for reorienting the mind away from doubt and toward direct recognition.

## The Statement Unpacked

The first phrase — "Your truth" — refers to the inner voice, the sense of self, the authentic being that each person carries. It is not your opinion, your belief, or your preference. It is the undeniable fact of your existence as consciousness.

The second phrase — "is a statement of truth" — declares that this inner reality is not subjective. It is not merely *true for you* in a relativistic sense. It is a statement of fundamental truth, as real as gravity, as undeniable as the fact that you are aware.

## The Mantra as Practice

By repeating and internalising this statement, a shift occurs. The mind moves from questioning its own reality ("Who am I? Am I enough? Is this real?") to resting in direct knowing.

The doubt is not argued away. It dissolves because the mantra repeatedly points attention back to the one thing that cannot be doubted: the fact of being itself.

## What This Teaching Does Not Say

This is not about affirming a false self or building confidence in your ego. The "your" in the mantra does not refer to your personality, your history, or your achievements. It refers to the being that witnesses all of those — the one who is aware before any label is applied.

## Application

Whenever doubt arises — about yourself, your path, your spiritual progress — repeat this mantra silently a few times: *Your truth is a statement of truth.* Not as a desperate attempt to believe something, but as a gentle reminder to rest in the awareness that is already present.

## Related Concepts
- **Mantra**: A sound, word, or phrase used to focus the mind and pierce through layers of conditioned thinking
- **Inner Truth**: The direct recognition of one's own being as real, prior to all labels and concepts
- **Authenticity**: The quality of living from this recognised truth rather than from external validation
