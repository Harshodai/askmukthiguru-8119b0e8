## Summary
This teaching explores the affirmation "Your truth is a statement of truth" as a powerful mantra for embracing one's authentic self. The core wisdom emphasizes that the inner voice or sense of self is not merely a label but an expression of fundamental truth that resides within each individual. By internalizing this affirmation, one can transcend doubt and fully embody the authenticity of their being.

## Key Teachings
- The phrase "Your truth is a statement of truth" serves as a mantra that affirms the inherent reality of one's own identity and inner voice.
- The self ("your") is not merely a label but an expression of fundamental truth that is undeniable and present within you.
- By repeating and internalizing this statement, you can move beyond doubt and embrace the authenticity of your being.

## Quotes
> "Your truth is a statement of truth." — Sri Preethaji & Sri Krishnaji

## Related Concepts
- Mantra: A repetitive phrase used as a spiritual tool to focus the mind and internalize truth.
- Authenticity: The quality of being true to one's own self, free from external doubt or imposition.
