# The First Sacred Secret — Live with a Spiritual Vision

Sri Preethaji opens the first of the Four Sacred Secrets with a teaching that sits at the foundation of all transformation: which state of being is carrying you determines the quality of your life.

## State Determines Flow

Most people believe that life is determined by circumstances — what happens to them, what others do, what the economy does. The first sacred secret says otherwise: your state of being is the primary factor. It is not what happens to you that shapes your life — it is the state from which you meet what happens.

A person who lives from a stressed state, regardless of their external success, will experience life as struggle. A person who lives from a beautiful state, regardless of external challenges, will experience life as flow.

## Spiritual Vision as Transformer

Opening the heart and absorbing these sacred secrets shifts the flow of life toward the miraculous. This is not hyperbole. When consciousness is transformed, perception shifts. What appeared as obstacles become opportunities. What seemed random becomes meaningful. The person operating from transformed consciousness becomes a conscious wealth creator, an awakened parent, a heartful partner, and a joyful individual — not by adding these roles, but by being the kind of person these roles express naturally.

## Consciousness-Based Living

The four sacred secrets are not techniques to be applied. They are attunements to the power of consciousness. Reading them as a daily meditation practice helps anchor this shift. They provide clarity during life's many challenges not by solving problems, but by reminding you of the state from which all problems are met differently.

## Application

Read one section of the Four Sacred Secrets each day as part of your meditation practice. Do not analyse it. Do not try to apply it. Simply absorb it. Let the shift in consciousness occur on its own.
