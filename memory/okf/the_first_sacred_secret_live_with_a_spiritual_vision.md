# The First Sacred Secret: Live with a Spiritual Vision

## Summary
The First Sacred Secret teaches that living with a spiritual vision transforms consciousness and shifts life's flow toward the miraculous. Sri Preethaji and Sri Krishnaji present this as the foundational principle for overcoming stress and anxiety, enabling practitioners to become conscious wealth creators, awakened parents, heartful partners, and joyful individuals who function from a transformed state of consciousness.

## Key Teachings
- **State of Being Determines Life Flow**: Which state of being is carrying you determines the quality of your life experiences and outcomes.
- **Spiritual Vision Creates Transformation**: Opening hearts and absorbing these sacred secrets causes the flow of life to shift toward the miraculous.
- **Consciousness-Based Living**: The four sacred secrets are designed to attune individuals to the power of consciousness, creating prosperity and love for ourselves, loved ones, and the world around us.
- **Daily Practice Integration**: Reading sections as part of a daily meditation practice helps find clarity during life's many daily challenges.

## Quotes
> "So open your hearts. Even as you read each of these sacred secrets and absorb them, you will see the flow of your life shifting toward the miraculous." — Sri Preethaji
