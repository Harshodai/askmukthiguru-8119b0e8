# Feeling One with Universal Intelligence

We all need to become awakened human beings. Enlightenment is the highest state a human being can attain. If the outer world's highest goal is to reach Mars, the inner world's highest goal is to reach an enlightened state — which means we have to learn to be free of suffering.

Sri Preethaji and Sri Krishnaji teach that an awakened person lives in beautiful, powerful, transcendental states where they feel completely one with everything — one with universal intelligence. Separation is gone. Division has ended. There is only oneness.

## Who Creates Division

This is the sharpest edge of the teaching: every division and every separation we see in this world is created by us. Not by God, not by karma, not by fate. By us — from a suffering state, not knowing how to live in an enlightened state.

The more we divide, the more we need peace. Divided within yourself, you need peace. Divided within your family, you need peace. Divided as nations, you need peace. The very search for peace is a symptom of the division we have created.

## The Lesson Universal Intelligence Teaches

When the Covid crisis forced humanity into isolation, people were forced to stay with family, with themselves — the very things they had been escaping from through work and pleasure. The universal intelligence had decided: let humanity learn to spend time with humanity.

But because the state of peace was absent, what emerged was not connection but crisis — a rise in domestic violence, addiction, and conflict. This is the hard truth: being together does not create peace. Only peace creates peace.

## Application

The path to feeling one with universal intelligence begins not with grand spiritual experiences but with a single admission: *I am the one who creates division in my own mind.* Seeing this — not as guilt but as clarity — is the first step. The second is learning to rest in a beautiful state long enough that the drive to divide falls away on its own.

## Related Concepts
- **Moksha**: Liberation — the end of the illusion of separation
- **Beautiful State**: The inner condition from which oneness is naturally experienced
- **Universal Intelligence**: The field of consciousness that we are never separate from — only unaware of
