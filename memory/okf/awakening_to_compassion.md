## Summary
This teaching explores the journey from experiencing states of calmness and presence to awakening to compassion and feeling deeply connected to others. It describes how universal intelligence can flow into a person when they allow themselves to be in a state of inner stillness, leading to a transformative experience of compassion and connection.

## Key Teachings
- A person can experience states of calmness and being present, which serve as the foundation for spiritual awakening.
- Universal intelligence can be allowed to flow into a person when they are in a state of inner stillness and openness.
- One can awaken to compassion as a natural result of being in a state of calm presence.
- When in this awakened state, one can feel connected to others, transcending the sense of separation.

## Quotes
> "One can awaken to compassion." — Sri Krishnaji

## Related Concepts
- Beautiful State: A state of connection, peace, and inner joy, free from suffering.
- Oneness: The concept of unity and connection with all beings.
- Power of Consciousness: The potential for human transformation through awareness.
- Inner Stillness: The state of calmness and presence that allows universal intelligence to flow.
