# Serene Mind — a 3-minute practice

Sri Krishnaji's concise practice for returning to calm whenever distracted,
disturbed, or preoccupied with past or future.

## When to use

- Whenever you are distracted and disturbed, do the serene mind.
- Whenever you are preoccupied with the past or the future, do the serene mind.
- Whenever you want to return to calm, do the serene mind.
- It is three minutes to a serene state of mind.

## Steps

1. **Sit still, spine erect.** Gently close your eyes.
2. **Conscious breathing** for three cycles of breath. Let your exhalation be
   longer than your inhalation.
3. **Recognize the emotion** arising within you now. Is it irritation, anxiety,
   or confusion — or is it joy, gratitude, or love?
4. **Observe the direction of your thinking.** Is it entangled in the past or
   the future, or is it in the present?
5. **Move your attention to your eyebrow center.** Visualize or feel a tiny
   flame. Move from your eyebrow center into the middle of your brain. Hold
   your attention on this flame in the middle of your brain for a few moments.
6. **When ready, smile and slowly open your eyes.** Namaste.
